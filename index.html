Singapore

beautiful modern first tier country. Besides the interesting contrast in the distribution of the districts -- the sci-fi feeling CBD,
lovely old town roads in China town and little India.. the more underlaying parts of the country, its history, politics and economics (as how
does such a small country which is totally lack of natural resources, becoems so successful and continuing prospering), are equally
fascinating to me! In this website, I will give you a simple introduction to this amazing island country.
